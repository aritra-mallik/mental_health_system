Frequently Asked Questions

Is this therapy? +

No. SupportSync is a digital support and awareness platform. It is not a replacement for professional clinical care, therapy, or medical advice. Think of it as a compassionate first step.

Is my data safe? +

Absolutely. We utilize zero-knowledge architecture. All conversations and journals are encrypted locally on your device using AES-256-GCM before syncing.

What is the WHO-5 assessment? +

The WHO-5 Well-Being Index is a scientifically validated, 5-item questionnaire developed by the World Health Organization to screen for depression and gauge overall emotional well-being. Scores range from 0 to 100 — higher is better.

Is it free to use? +

Yes — core features including the mood check-in and WHO-5 assessment are completely free.

Can I use it anonymously? +

Yes. You can explore most features without creating an account. Sign up only if you'd like to save your mood history over time.

Important Notice
